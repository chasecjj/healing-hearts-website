**From:** Chase

**Re:** Copy polish across all 5 print pieces before April 10 send-to-print

## The Problem

The magazine ad and brand banner are currently saying the same thing in slightly different formats. Key phrases — "reignite intimacy and trust," "identify and transform subconscious barriers," "profound sense of safety," "connection with your partner" — appear on both. If someone sees both at the expo, they read the same message twice.

Each piece should have **one job**:

| Piece | Job | Lifespan |
|-------|-----|----------|
| **Magazine Ad** | Drive QR scans from people NOT at the booth yet | Expo-specific |
| **Brand Banner** (existing retractable) | "Who is Healing Hearts?" — brand authority, stops foot traffic | **Evergreen** (reuse at future events) |
| **Challenge Banner** (new 2x6ft) | Sell the 7-Day Challenge, drive signups | Campaign-specific |
| **Flyer** (booth takeaway) | Remind them later when they're home — the "substance" piece | Evergreen-ish |
| **Magnet** (booth takeaway) | Stay on their fridge for weeks. Fridge billboard. | Evergreen |

---

## Specific Changes & Copy Per Piece

### 1. Magazine Ad

**Keep:** Headline ("TRANSFORM YOUR CONNECTION"), $1000 date-night prize callout, 7-Day Challenge CTA, QR code. These are all strong.

**Change the body copy.** Must mention "coaching" so people know what Trisha does.

Current:
> Ready to reignite intimacy and trust? Our expert relationship coaching helps you identify and transform subconscious barriers, creating a profound sense of safety and connection with your partner.

Replace with one of these options (Desirae to pick):

**Option A — Coaching upfront:**
> **What if the thing keeping you apart isn't what you think it is?** Our couples coaching helps you discover the hidden patterns — and change them for good.

**Option B — Coaching as the answer:**
> **What if the thing keeping you apart isn't what you think it is?** Relationship coaching that uncovers the real patterns and rewires them — together.

**Option C — Shortest:**
> **What if the thing keeping you apart isn't what you think it is?** Expert couples coaching to find the hidden patterns — and change them.

**Why:** Shorter, curiosity-driven (pulls the reader in instead of explaining), and doesn't repeat any language from the banners. Magazine ads get ~2 seconds of attention — a question hooks better than a service description.

### 2. Brand Banner (Existing Retractable — Evergreen)

This banner needs to work at any event, not just this expo. It should answer: *What is this? Who's behind it? Where do I learn more?*

**Keep:** Headline ("HEAL YOUR HEARTS. REIGNITE YOUR LOVE."), subtitle ("Trusted Guidance For a Love that Lasts!"), Trisha's headshot, the three icon pillars (Understand Your Patterns / Transform Your Story / Rekindle Your Joy) — these are great visual hierarchy and work long-term.

**Fix typo:** "reinew" → "renew" (in the bullet section).

**Replace the entire bullet section** with Trisha's positioning. The three pillars already communicate what the coaching does. The bullets below them repeat the same ideas in different words. Since the Challenge now has its own banner, this space should build credibility instead:

Current:
> Ready to reinew intimacy and trust? Our expert relationship coaching will help you:
> - Identify and transform subconscious barriers
> - Create a profound sense of safety
> - Establish lasting connection with your partner.

Replace with:
> **Trisha Johnson**
> Relationship Coach & Speaker
>
> - 20+ years as a wellness coach — now specializing in couples
> - 5 proprietary frameworks rooted in neuroscience
> - 38 years of marriage — she coaches what she's lived
> - Speaker, workshop leader, and program creator
>
> [QR Code]
> **healingheartscourse.com**

**Also update the bottom CTA.** "FIND OUT MORE ON OUR WEBSITE" → just **healingheartscourse.com** with the QR. No instruction needed — people know what QR codes do.

**Why:** At any future event, the banner answers three questions in order: *What is this?* (headline + pillars), *Who's behind it?* (Trisha's name + credential bullets), *Where do I learn more?* (QR). Works whether the Challenge exists or not. Clean, timeless.

### 3. Challenge Banner (New 2x6ft — Campaign-Specific)

Single-purpose: get people to scan and sign up for the 7-Day Challenge. Every word earns its spot.

**Top (headline zone):**
> **7 Days. 10 Minutes.**
> **One Relationship — Transformed.**

**Middle (what it is):**
> A free guided challenge for couples.
> One simple daily practice — together.
>
> No cost. No commitment. Just connection.

**Bottom (CTA zone):**
> [Large QR Code]
> **Scan to Start — Free**
>
> Healing Hearts logo
> *Regulate. Rewire. Reignite.*

**Design notes:**
- QR code should be BIG — scannable from 4+ feet away on a 2x6 banner
- Warm couple photo if there's room, but copy > imagery on this one
- "Free" does heavy lifting at an expo — make it visually prominent
- Teal/coral brand palette but keep it clean — this banner needs to read fast from a distance
- Consider placing this banner on the booth table or at the front edge where people approach

### 4. Flyer (Booth Takeaway)

This is where all the coaching details belong — the stuff we're pulling OFF the other pieces. People take this home and read it when they're deciding whether to sign up.

**Suggested copy structure:**

**Front:**
> **Your Relationship Has a Pattern. Let's Change It.**
>
> Most couples know something feels off — but can't pinpoint why.
> That's because the real barriers aren't on the surface.
> They're in the subconscious stories you've carried since childhood.
>
> Healing Hearts helps you:
> - **Identify the hidden patterns** keeping you stuck
> - **Build real safety** so vulnerability feels natural, not terrifying
> - **Create lasting connection** that doesn't fade when life gets hard
>
> Led by Trisha Johnson — relationship coach, speaker, and creator of the Healing Hearts method.

**Back:**
> **Start Free: The 7-Day Couples Challenge**
>
> A guided daily practice you do together — 10 minutes a day, 7 days.
> No cost. No commitment. Just one small step toward each other.
>
> [QR Code]
> healingheartscourse.com
>
> [Trisha headshot + Healing Hearts logo]

**Why:** This is the only piece where someone will actually sit down and read. Give them the substance here — what the coaching does, why it works, who Trisha is. The front sells the problem, the back sells the free entry point.

### 5. Magnet (Fridge Keepsake)

Magnets work because people don't throw them away. They sit on the fridge for months. The copy needs to work at a glance from 3 feet away, weeks after the expo.

**Suggested copy (entire magnet):**

> **Healing Hearts**
> *Regulate. Rewire. Reignite.*
>
> **Your love is worth fighting for.**
>
> [QR Code]
> Free 7-Day Couples Challenge

That's it. Logo, one emotional line, QR, and what they'll get when they scan. No bullet points. No paragraphs. Fridge billboard.

**Why:** A magnet isn't a flyer — it's a reminder. It needs to trigger the thought "oh right, that couples thing from the expo" three weeks later. One line does that better than a paragraph.
